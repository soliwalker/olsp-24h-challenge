Part 2: The #1 Mistake to AVOID (The "Commission Killer")

The single biggest mistake beginners make is "Anonymous Link Dropping."

This is when you post your affiliate link everywhere (Facebook comments, forums, random messages) with no context, no story, and no personality. It looks like spam, it feels like spam, and it gets ignored.

THE FIX: Always "Warm-Up" Your Link!

Never just post the link by itself. Instead, use a simple script like the one below to give it context and make people want to click. Your goal is to share a result or a journey, not just an advertisement.
